Our Story

Born from a passion for exceptional quality and French culinary tradition, Maison Fresh brings artisanal excellence to Toronto's most historic market.

French Heritage, Toronto Excellence

Founded by Chef Antoine Dubois, formerly of Le Bernardin and Daniel, Maison Fresh represents three generations of French market expertise. Our commitment to sourcing the finest seasonal produce from local Ontario farms and international artisans ensures every item meets our exacting standards.

Located in the heart of St Lawrence Market, we honor Toronto's rich culinary history while introducing the sophisticated French approach to fresh market shopping. Every product is carefully curated, every relationship with our suppliers built on trust and shared passion for excellence.
